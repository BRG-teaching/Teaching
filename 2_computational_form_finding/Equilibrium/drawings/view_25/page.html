Description
Move the handles to explore the possible configurations of the tree structure in the form diagram. Observe the implications on the distribution of both reaction forces and internal forces in the force diagram. In mode 1, follow the construction process: 1) balancing the resultant of the overall system by the trunk; 2-4) redistributing the load to three tiers of two, four and eight branches and drawing the corresponding force diagram.
